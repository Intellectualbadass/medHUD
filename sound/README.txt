medhud
=======

CUSTOMIZATION

-Edit the resource\ClientScheme.res for colors! Make MedHUD your own!

-For a killfeed where it is on most HUDs, go to the scripts\hudlayout.res and control-F for "HudDeathNotice" and change the "ypos" value to "12". 
-For a lowered killfeed change the "ypos" value to "375"

-To get rid of the red directional damage lines, go to scripts\hudlayout.res and control-F for "HudDamageIndicator" and change "visible" and "enabled" to "0".
